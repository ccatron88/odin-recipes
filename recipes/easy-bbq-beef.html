Easy BBQ Beef

Description

BBQ beef is a favorite for many people. However, we don't all have the time to fire up the smoker and tend to a roast for hours at a time! Use this easy crockpot recipe to create a great meal for the family without the hassle of firing up the smoker!

Ingredients

  • One Beef Roast
  • One cup of water
  • One beef bouillon cube
  • Two tablespoons of brown sugar
  • Two teaspoons of liquid smoke
  • One tablespoon of paprika
  • One teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • One teaspoon of chili powder
  • One teaspoon of salt
  • One teaspoon of onion powder
  • One teaspoon of garlic powder
  • One-fourth teaspoon of cumin
  • One-eighth teaspoon of black pepper
  • Two teaspoons of Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in crockpot.
  2. Add beef roast to mixture.
  3. Cook on high for 3-4 hours and then another 1-2 hours on low.
  4. Take the roast out and set aside.
  5. Take two cups of liquid mixture and set aside.
  6. Drain the rest of the liquid content.
  7. Shredd the roast to your liking.
  8. Add shredded beef back to crockpot and add BBQ sauce to your liking.
  9. While adding BBQ sauce, add a little bit of the set aside liquid mixture to your liking.
