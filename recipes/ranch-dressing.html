Ranch Dressing

Description

Red Beans and Rice is a traditional southern comfort-style food. Great for large gatherings and filling you up! The three key ingredients are rice, beans, and some good ol' Andouille sausage. Add some hot sauce of your choosing and you have a homerun dinner meal!

Ingredients

  • One whole Green Pepper
  • Two tablespoons of minced garlic
  • One-half large yellow or sweet onion
  • 1lb. of dried red beans, soaked overnight
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • One and one-half tablespoons of Tony Cachere's seasoning
  • One-third tsp of cinnamon
  • One teaspoon dried mustard powder
  • One teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Seven to eight cups of water
  • Two chicken boullion
  • One ham steak
  • One to two packs of Andouille sausage (to liking)
  • Two bay leaves

Steps

  1. Sautee green bell pepper, onion, and minced garlic with olive oil in a pan.
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients together in a crockpot.
  3. Add sauteed items to crockpot.
  4. Let cook 4-6 hours on high or 8-10 hours on low.
  5. Remove and throw away the bay leaves from the crockpot.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
