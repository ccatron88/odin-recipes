Ranch Dressing

Description

Every meal needs a little something to take it to the next level. Or sometimes, you're just looking for a little flavor for an appetizer or snack! Ranch dressing is the side that never fails. Follow this recipe for a great homemade ranch dressing!

Ingredients

  • One cup of Mayo
  • One-half cup of sour cream
  • One-half cup of buttermilk
  • One packet of Hidden Valley Restaurant Style Ranch
  • One teaspoon of fresh garlic minced (or jarred from the store if that's what you have!)
  • One teaspoon of minced chives
  • One-half teaspoon of paprika (optional)
  • One teaspoon of dill weed if you're looking for some extra pizazz (my personal favorite)

Steps

  1. Simply mix all the ingredients in a large bowl and enjoy!
